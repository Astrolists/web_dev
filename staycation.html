Palisade State Park

About Palisade State Park

Palisade State Park is a state park in central UT, north of St. George that offers many activities such as hiking, boating, and more. It is an ideal destination for many families due to range of activities & diverse wildlife. Many agree, calling it the best state park in Utah!

Activities to do

palisade State Park offers many activities such as hiking, boating, kayaking/canoeing, swimming in the beautiful, crystal clear waters, hunting, birdwatching and observing wildlife, taking a drive through local scenery

Palisade State Park offers $100 cabins for people to stay in! this is an excellent deal as the cabins are large enough to hold families and are available for multi-day stays to Palisade. Make sure to get one before they sell out for the summer!

Reasons you Should Visit

  1. It's only $15 to get in
  2. You can go camping or fishing
  3. It has some of the best golf courses in Utah
  4. It offers amazing mountains nearby for hiking
