Death Note

Death Note Encyclopedia

Death Note is a psychological thriller manga by Tsugumi Ohba from the time period of Dec. 2003-May 2006.

Beginning Summary With Spoilers

Death note begins the story with Ryuk, a Shinigami (A God of Death in Japanese), dropping a "Death Note" from the Shinigami realm into the human world. Entering this, a 17 year old by the name of Light Yagami, a highschooler in Japan, finds and picks it up; initially thinking it was a joke. Shortly after this, he writes the name of a criminal on live television, and 40 seconds after this, he is reported dead.

Spoiler Free Summary

A smart yet disenchanted student Light Yagami finds a mysterious notebook one day in a school courtyard. He quickly finds out this book can end the life of anyone whose name he writes upon the pages of said book. Following this, an investigation is put forth after the mysterious deaths of many criminals.
